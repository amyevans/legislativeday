Speakers

Four speakers sure to transform your attitude and your business

Anthony Morris

Morris has spent the last 25 years through 50 countries speaking for and coaching groups of financial advisors. He is a pioneer in client building for financial professionals and an evangelist for industry transformation. Anthony’s message is guaranteed to jolt you from your comfort zone and transform the way you view and do your business in all areas from prospecting and client referrals to upselling and technology deployment.

Bill Grimes

Have you wondered why you hesitate to call or contact potential clients? Have you felt your level of tension, stress, or anxiety increase as you think of contacting certain individuals or organizations? Grimes will introduce you to the 12 different types of fear that cause this anxiety and arm you with the knowledge to overcome these barriers to increasing business. A former insurance agent himself, Bill has experienced first-hand the ups and downs of building a business and is passionate about helping individuals overcome Call Reluctance® to reach their potential.

Matt Jones

Jones is a three-time cancer survivor, professional speaker, author, and founder of the Real Leadership Academy. Through his against all odds story of conquering cancer, relearning how to walk, and running a marathon, Matt shares strategies to turn challenges into opportunities and excel to new heights of performance.

To Be Announced

The final speaker will keynote Tuesday's Legislative Luncheon before a packed house of Texas legislators and insurance and financial professionals from across the state.

FAQ

Your questions, answered

Whether it is your first time attending a NAIFA event or your thirtieth, get all the information you need to have a successful conference.

Should I book my own legislative appointments?

No! While in past years you may have scheduled your own appointment(s), the NAIFA-Texas staff will be scheduling all appointments this year. Please do not contact your legislator's office to avoid double-booking.

However, once you have been emailed your appointment information, we do encourage you to confirm the appointment with your legislator, particularly if you already have a relationship with him or her.

What should I wear?

Professional dress is appropriate for Tuesday's legislative appointments and luncheon. Business casual dress may be worn the remainder of the conference.

Will talking points be provided?

Yes, talking points and leave-behind materials will be provided and will also be discussed at Tuesday's Legislative Briefing Breakfast. If you would like to prepare for your legislative appointments in other ways, research your legislator's background and committee assignments.

Can nonmembers attend?

Absolutely!
